Mango Sticky Rice

Description

In for a Thai dessert? This one is for you. Sweet, salty, and simple to make, this dish brings the best of what mango and rice have to offer.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Thai sweet rice
  • 1 ripe mango
  • coconut milk
  • dried coconut flakes

Steps

  1. Cook the rice in water
  2. Add the coconut milk and some brown sugar and cook some more
  3. Slice the mango into cubes. Serve the rice with the mango cubes and coconut flakes on top.
