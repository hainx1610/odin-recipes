Pho Ga

Description

My personal favorite dish! Vietnamese cuisine arguably does not get better than this. If you're a fan of chicken and/or soup, give this one a shot.

Ingredients

  • whole chicken
  • rice noodles
  • cilantro
  • thai basil
  • green onion
  • ginger
  • lime
  • mint

Steps

  1. Char onion and ginger
  2. Make the broth by adding the whole chicken together with the charred onion and ginger in water and simmer for 90 minutes
  3. Strain, shred chicken, and serve with the rest of the ingredients as toppings
