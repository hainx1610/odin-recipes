Mango Sticky Rice

Description

You might think this recipe is redundant, but being able to perfectly hard-boil eggs is no minor feat. Espcially if you can't seem to peel them easily.

Ingredients

  • egg(s) (as many as you want)
  • water (in a pan)

Steps

  1. Boil the water first! Boiling the egg(s) in cold water will make the membrane stick to the shell.
  2. After a rolling boil, bring the water to a simmer. Add the egg(s).
  3. Close the lid and wait for 8 minutes (6 if soft-boiled is what you're after).
  4. Immediately move the egg(s) to an ice bath or simply a bowl of cold water to stop the cooking process.
  5. Peel the egg(s), first by cracking the top on a flat surface, then remove the rest of the shell.
  6. Voila! Enjoy your egg(s).
